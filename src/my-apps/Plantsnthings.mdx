The app was adapted from a [JS course](https://www.udemy.com/course/javascript-beginners-complete-tutorial/). Forms are validated manually, cookies allow users to add items to a cart without signing in. Encryption lets admins securely create an account and add products and images.

Product data, users, shopping carts and images are stored in JSON.

## Key skills

- Node, Express, HTML templating
- Cookie-session (Node module), user management
- Encryption, secure passwords (salting and hashing)
- Heroku CLI & hosting
